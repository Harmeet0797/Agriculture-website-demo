We are B.L Agro

An unwavering dedication and passion towards delivering the best and a closely knit relationship with nature has helped B.L.Agro develop brands that have lasted well over the years. An edible oil journey that began with a promise of delivering quality and purity-rich mustard oil, and a firm belief in the power of nature’s goodness, gave birth to Bail Kolhu Kacchi Ghani Mustard Oil. With sheer hard work and continuous research and development, the brand is today a flagship product of B L Agro Industries Ltd. Check this to read extra super jackpot. At our technologically advanced refinery plant, all processes are designed to be automated, right from the unloading of crude oil to the filling and packaging of our product in various pack sizes. The entire product life cycle is untouched by hand and processed through a stringent quality checklist that assures, that all procedures are safe and purest possible. Our journey did not stop at oil.The second breakthrough was in the food range with the launch of our Chakki Fresh Atta .The product became synonymous with the brand Nourish and over the years, we have expanded to a complete food cycle ranging from pulses, rice, ready to cooks to even dry fruits.
